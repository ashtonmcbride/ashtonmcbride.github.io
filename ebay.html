EBAY STORE

Personal Project

Description

I started my own eBay store after noticing I owned a considerable amount of valuable items in video games I played in the past. Since I no longer played them, I had no interest in keeping the items I owned. So, I ended up creating multiple listings of highly coveted items that people could buy from my store, making a couple thousand dollars in less than a few weeks.

Recently, my store has been rated a "Top Rated Seller" by eBay, meaning I am one of eBay's most reputable sellers. I consistently deliver outstanding customer service to my customers.
