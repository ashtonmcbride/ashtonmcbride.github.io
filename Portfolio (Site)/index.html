ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Ashton McBride.

I am a creative and innovative programmer, graphic designer, and video editor with a passion for storytelling, world design, and problem solving. Seeking to join a forward-thinking team to contribute 10+ years of online experience. Involved with many aspects of online game development, whether it's testing games, writing code, or working on level and character design.

After having completed an Associate Degree in Game Development - Programming, I hope to be an asset to a company that will allow me to be creative and share my ideas that will benefit them. I am able to collaborate well with others, bring ideas to life, and hope to one day create immersive experiences that will resonate with players.

My Mission Statement:

"To inspire creativity and immerse others through collaboration and imagination."
