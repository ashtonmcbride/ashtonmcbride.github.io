OLDER PROJECTS

BLUE HAT STUDIOS

❮
❯
1/7

Blue Hat Studios was a building group I founded back in 2016. I used to host build competitions in the group's primary game, BHS Build. Players would be able to join in and build anything they wanted using something known as F3X, a popular building tool at the time. BHS Build was where many people first learned how to build, and I ended up teaching hundreds of people how to get started with building on Roblox.

COIN QUEST

❮
❯
1/9

Coin Quest was essentially my first attempt at making a game. My idea for this game was for it to be a series of minigames that had something to do with collecting coins. However, because I did not know how to code, this game was more of a hangout game where I teased new features that were never implemented.
