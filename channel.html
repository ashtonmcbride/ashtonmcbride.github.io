Personal Project

Description

"The Council" is a YouTube channel made up of myself and a few of my close friends. Each member of The Council has a unique personality that when combined together, makes for a really fun time.

After the success of my eBay store (which can be seen here), I was able to get the necessary equipment to start recording my friends and I playing games. We thought that viewers might enjoy our witty humor and find our gameplay entertaining. Prior to starting our YouTube channel, I had a bit of video editing experience. However, I was happy to have another opportunity to continue practicing my video editing skills. I was also able to practice my graphic design skills by creating thumbnails for each video.

Although this YouTube channel is relatively new, I hope to keep uploading videos on this channel and grow a community that enjoys funny content.

Channel Page: https://youtube.com/@TheCouncil-YT
